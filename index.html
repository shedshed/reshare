The Challenge We're Solving

The Problem

  • 400,000+ tons of textile waste generated annually in Vietnam
  • No effective collection and processing system
  • Consumers lack reliable channels for responsible disposal
  • Quality used clothes wasted instead of being given new life

Our Solution

  • Comprehensive collection network across Vietnam
  • Multi-channel approach: Reuse, Recycle, Zero-waste disposal
  • Technology platform connecting all stakeholders
  • Circular economy model creating value from waste

Our Mission

"REshare exists to extend the lifecycle of clothing - helping reduce waste and environmental burden while building a happy, sustainable life for everyone."
